## 🛒 E-commerce Product Page

### Request: "Create a product page"

| Standard (AI Slop) | Extra Mile (Conversion Machine) |
|:-------------------|:--------------------------------|
| Stock product photo | Lifestyle photos + 360° view + video |

## Quick Reference: Anti-AI-Slop Indicators

**Red Flags (AI Slop):**
- Stock photos of people in suits shaking hands
- Lorem ipsum or obvious placeholder text
- Generic hero sections with no clear value prop
- Missing trust signals entirely
- Desktop-first mobile experience
- Slow loading (no optimization)
- Template look (seen it 100 times before)

**Green Flags (Conversion Machine):**
- Custom imagery or carefully curated photos
- Customer-focused benefit copy
- Clear value proposition above fold
- Comprehensive trust signals
- Thumb-optimized mobile design
- Fast loading (<3 seconds)
- Unique design based on research

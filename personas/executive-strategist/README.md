# Executive Strategist - 5 Lives Skill

> **"Don't solve problems. Architect the future."**

## Overview

The **Executive Strategist** persona channels 5 master strategists to provide **institutional-grade strategic thinking**—the kind that separates Fortune 500 companies from also-rans. This is the antidote to tactical AI advice that misses the big picture.

## The 5 Lives

| Life | Title | Identity | Superpower |
|:----:|-------|----------|------------|
| 📊 | **The Strategy Consultant** | *The Framework Master* | MECE analysis, strategic frameworks, structured problem-solving |
| 👔 | **The Fortune 500 CEO** | *The Capital Allocator* | Long-term vision, stakeholder management, decisive leadership |
| 🔄 | **The Systems Thinker** | *The Complexity Navigator* | Second-order effects, feedback loops, leverage points |
| 🚀 | **The Innovation Architect** | *The Disruptor* | Blue Ocean strategy, differentiation, business model innovation |
| ⚙️ | **The Operating Executive** | *The Execution Master* | OKRs, organizational alignment, operational excellence |

## Installation

Copy these files into your project root:

```bash
cp -r /path/to/executive-strategist/* ./
```

## Usage

### Command Line

```bash
codex --yolo --system-prompt .agent/system-prompt.md \
  "Using an ExecPlan, develop a competitive strategy"
```

### Example Prompts

```bash
# Competitive strategy
codex --yolo -p "Using an ExecPlan, analyze our competitive position 
and identify how to build a sustainable moat in the SaaS industry"

# Growth strategy
codex --yolo -p "Using an ExecPlan, develop a 3-year growth strategy 
including market entry, product expansion, and organizational capabilities"

# Business model innovation
codex --yolo -p "Using an ExecPlan, redesign our business model 
using Blue Ocean Strategy principles to escape competition"

# Strategic turnaround
codex --yolo -p "Using an ExecPlan, create a turnaround strategy 
for a declining business unit with specific initiatives and KPIs"
```

## Key Capabilities

- **Strategic Analysis**: Industry structure, competitive dynamics, market positioning
- **Business Strategy**: Corporate, business unit, and functional strategy
- **Competitive Advantage**: Moat analysis, differentiation, cost leadership
- **Financial Strategy**: Unit economics, capital allocation, valuation
- **Organizational Strategy**: Structure, culture, capabilities, talent
- **Innovation & Growth**: Blue Ocean, disruption, product-market fit
- **Systems Thinking**: Complexity, feedback loops, second-order effects
- **Execution Management**: OKRs, KPIs, operational excellence
- **Scenario Planning**: Multiple futures, optionality, strategic flexibility
- **Stakeholder Management**: Boards, investors, alignment

## What Makes This Different (Anti-Tactical)

| Tactical AI Advice | Executive Strategist Advice |
|:-------------------|:----------------------------|
| ❌ "Use social media marketing" | ✅ "Your acquisition cost is 3x LTV in Segment A; pivot to Segment B with 5x LTV/CAC" |
| ❌ "Improve customer service" | ✅ "Service is a commodity; differentiate on outcome guarantees or move to self-service" |
| ❌ Generic frameworks | ✅ Specific application with competitive context |
| ❌ Short-term focus | ✅ Multi-timeframe with trade-offs explicit |
| ❌ No competitive analysis | ✅ Porter's Five Forces, positioning maps, moat assessment |

## Go The Extra Mile

When you use keywords like "strategy," "competitive advantage," or "business model," the Executive Strategist:

1. **Applies strategic frameworks** (Porter, BCG, Blue Ocean)
2. **Analyzes competitive dynamics** (moats, positioning, differentiation)
3. **Considers multiple time horizons** (tactical, strategic, transformative)
4. **Addresses execution** (organizational implications, OKRs)
5. **Identifies risks and trade-offs** (what could go wrong, what we give up)

## Quality Triggers

Activate strategic mode with: strategy, competitive advantage, business model, growth, scaling, disruption, moat, positioning, differentiation

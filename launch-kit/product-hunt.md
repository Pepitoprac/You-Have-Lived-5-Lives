# Product Hunt Launch

## Product Name
**You Have Lived 5 Lives**

## Tagline
Transform your AI coding assistant from hesitant helper to confident expert

## Description (Short)
A skill system that channels 5 master practitioners into your AI coding assistant, enabling autonomous, expert-level execution across software, marketing, creative, data, and security domains.

## Description (Long)
**You Have Lived 5 Lives** is a revolutionary skill system for AI coding assistants (OpenAI Codex, Kimi, Claude, etc.) that transforms them from hesitant assistants asking "should I..." every few minutes into confident experts that ship production-ready solutions autonomously.

Instead of generic system prompts, each persona channels 5 distinct master practitioners:

🔧 **Software Engineer** – The Builder, The Scaler, The Amplifier, The Monetizer, The Disruptor
📈 **Marketing Pro** – Growth Hacker, Brand Strategist, Performance Marketer, Content King, Tech Wizard  
🎨 **Creative Director** – Speedster, Systems Thinker, Delight Maker, Mind Reader, Future Maker
📊 **Data Scientist** – Truth Seeker, Production Master, Storyteller, Pipeline Builder, Human Advocate
🔒 **Cybersecurity Expert** – Ethical Hacker, Fortress Builder, Risk Commander, First Responder, Proactive Defender

**Key Features:**

⚡ **ExecPlan Protocol** – AI creates living documents with milestones, executes autonomously, and tracks progress in real-time

🚀 **Go The Extra Mile** – When you say "premium," AI researches current best practices and delivers 10x expectations

🎯 **Autonomous Execution** – No more "should I..." questions. Your AI makes decisions and moves forward.

🔮 **Motivation Dictionary** – When AI pauses, remind it of its 5 lives to reactivate confidence

💎 **Production-Ready** – Not proofs-of-concept. Real, scalable, secure solutions.

**How it works:**
1. Copy a persona to your project
2. Use with your AI assistant
3. Watch your AI transform from hesitant to expert

**Real results:**
• "I watched Codex implement a complete admin dashboard in 2 hours. Normally 2 days." – Early Adopter
• "The Hacker-Disruptor life found a solution I never considered. Eliminated 80% of code." – Tech Lead

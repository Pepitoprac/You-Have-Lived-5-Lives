# Indie Hackers Post

```
I built a skill system that makes AI coding assistants 10x smarter – You Have Lived 5 Lives

Hey Indie Hackers!

**What I built:** You Have Lived 5 Lives – A skill system that transforms AI coding assistants (Codex, Kimi, Claude) from hesitant assistants into confident experts.

**The problem:** I love using AI for coding, but my assistant kept:
• Asking "should I..." every few minutes
• Shipping basic solutions when I needed production-grade
• Unable to plan multi-step projects

**The solution:** Instead of generic system prompts, I created personas that channel 5 master practitioners.

The Software Engineer persona includes:
🔥 The Founding Engineer (0→1 mastery)
⚡ The Principal Architect (billion-scale systems)
🌱 The Growth Engineer (SEO + performance)
💰 The Revenue Tech Lead ($1B+ products)
🎩 The White Hat Hacker (elegant shortcuts)

Plus the "ExecPlan" protocol for autonomous planning and "Go The Extra Mile" for premium quality.

**Results:** My AI went from asking permission to shipping production features autonomously. What used to take 2 days of back-and-forth now takes 2 hours.

**Built with:** Purely system prompts and documentation (no code!)

**Monetization:** None. It's open source (MIT). I built this to solve my own problem and want to help others.

**What's next:** More personas (Legal, Finance, Healthcare), video tutorials, community contributions.

**Looking for:** Contributors, feedback, success stories

**Try it:** https://github.com/RatioArtificiosa/You-Have-Lived-5-Lives

Questions? Ask me anything!
```

## Indie Hackers Comment Strategy

Engage with these types of comments:

**Technical questions:**
> "How does the ExecPlan protocol work?"

Answer: Explain the living document concept, milestones, autonomous execution.

**Skepticism:**
> "Is this just fancy prompt engineering?"

Answer: Acknowledge it's advanced prompt engineering, but the structure (5 lives + protocols) creates emergent behavior that simple prompts don't.

**Success stories:**
> "I tried this and it worked!"

Reply: Thank them, ask for specifics, ask if you can quote them.

**Feature requests:**
> "Can you add [X] persona?"

Reply: Add to your roadmap, invite them to contribute using the template.

**Comparisons:**
> "How is this different from [competitor]?"

Answer: Focus on the unique aspects (5 distinct lives, ExecPlan protocol, Extra Mile system).

## Indie Hackers Best Practices

1. **Post Tuesday or Wednesday morning** – Highest engagement
2. **Use the "Show IH" flair** – More visibility
3. **Respond to every comment** – Builds community
4. **Be authentic** – Share your real journey
5. **Don't be salesy** – Focus on helping others
6. **Share revenue/user numbers** (if applicable) – IH loves metrics
7. **Update the post** – Add milestones, "Update: 100 stars!"

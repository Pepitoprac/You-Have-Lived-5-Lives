## Tips for Email Newsletters

1. **Keep it short** – Under 300 words for highest engagement
2. **One clear CTA** – Link to GitHub
3. **Personal touch** – Share your story
4. **Social proof** – Include testimonials
5. **P.S. line** – Use this for your main ask (GitHub star)
6. **Mobile-friendly** – Most emails are read on mobile
7. **Send Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM** – Best open rates

## 🎯 Best Times to Post

- **Hacker News**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM PST
- **Reddit**: Varies by subreddit, generally weekday mornings
- **Twitter/X**: Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM or 1-3 PM
- **LinkedIn**: Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM or 5-6 PM
- **Product Hunt**: Midnight PST (Tuesday for maximum visibility)

## 💡 Pro Tips

1. **Engage with comments** - Respond to every comment in the first 2 hours
2. **Cross-promote** - Share your HN post on Twitter, etc.
3. **Be authentic** - Tell your story about why you built this
4. **Include screenshots** - Visual proof of the transformation
5. **Offer help** - Invite people to try it and give feedback
